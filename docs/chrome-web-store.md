## Detailed description
Stop scrolling past AI slop without knowing it. AI Fake Or Real tells you what's real and what's fake — right in your feed.

🥸 WHAT IS THIS?
An ad blocker blocks ads. AI Fake Or Real flags AI. It runs quietly in the background while you browse social media, marking AI-generated posts, images, and text so you always know what's real.

A small badge on the extension icon tells you at a glance:
  ✓ Green = Real — this content was made by a human
  ? Amber = Unclear — could be either, your vote helps decide
  AI Red = AI Fake — this was probably generated by AI

On social media, badges appear directly on posts in your feed. No clicking, no interruption.

👥 THE COMMUNITY DECIDES
Not sure about a post? See how others voted. Tap 👍 for Real or 👎 for AI Fake. When the scanner and the crowd disagree, you'll see an ⚡ alert — your vote helps settle it.

This isn't just an algorithm. It's a community of real people helping each other spot fakes.

📊 YOUR AI EXPOSURE
Ever wonder how much AI content you're actually seeing? AI Fake Or Real tracks it for you — over the last day, week, month, or year. Like screen time, but for AI content. (Stored locally on your device. We never see it.)

🌐 WORKS ON YOUR FAVORITE PLATFORMS
• Reddit — flags AI posts and art as you scroll
• Instagram — detects AI-generated photos and captions
• YouTube — checks video descriptions and comments
• Facebook — spots AI posts in your feed
• TikTok — flags AI content and comments
• X (Twitter) — detects bot-written tweets
• News sites, blogs, and any website

⚡ FAST & PRIVATE
• Runs instantly — the local scanner works offline, no waiting
• No data collected — AI exposure stats never leave your device
• No cookies, no tracking, no ads — ever
• Open source — see exactly what the code does: github.com/Mingling94/AIFakeOrReal

💰 100% FREE
No subscription. No word limits. No premium tier. Free forever, because everyone deserves to know what's real.

⌨️ KEYBOARD SHORTCUT
Press Cmd+Shift+A (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+A (Windows) to instantly check any page.

🤔 HOW IS THIS DIFFERENT FROM GPTZERO OR ORIGINALITY.AI?
Those tools are for teachers checking student essays and editors auditing articles. They require you to paste text into a website and pay $10-15/month.

AI Fake Or Real is for everyday browsing. It runs passively while you scroll your feed. It's free. And it includes community voting and AI exposure tracking — features nobody else has.
