## Detailed description
Stop scrolling past AI slop without knowing it. AI Fake Or Real tells you what's real and what's fake — right in your feed.

🥸 WHAT IS THIS?
An ad blocker blocks ads. AI Fake Or Real flags AI. It runs quietly in the background while you browse social media, marking AI-generated posts, images, and text so you always know what's real.

A small badge on the extension icon tells you at a glance:
  ✓ Green = Real — this content was made by a human
  ? Amber = Unclear — could be either, your vote helps decide
  AI Red = AI Fake — this was probably generated by AI

On social media, badges appear directly on posts in your feed. No clicking, no interruption.

👥 THE COMMUNITY DECIDES
Not sure about a post? See how others voted. Tap 👍 for Real or 👎 for AI Fake. When the scanner and the crowd disagree, you'll see an ⚡ alert — your vote helps settle it.

This isn't just an algorithm. It's a community of real people helping each other spot fakes.

Ever wonder how much AI content you're actually seeing? AI Fake Or Real tracks it for you — over the last day, week, month, or year. Like screen time, but for AI content. (Stored locally on your device. We never see it.)

## Privacy
- Data handling: AI exposure history and preferences are stored locally on the device and never transmitted. Page text/images are sent to the detection API only to compute a score.
- Privacy policy URL (use now — public + HTTPS): **https://github.com/Mingling94/AIFakeOrReal/blob/main/docs/privacy-policy.md**
  - Why not the Pages URL: Pages is enabled (`main` → `/docs`) but the user-level custom domain `mingluo.me` has no valid TLS cert ("Enforce HTTPS — Unavailable"), so it serves **http only** and `github.io` redirects to it. Chrome Web Store requires an **https** privacy URL.
  - To make `https://mingluo.me/AIFakeOrReal/privacy.html` work later: at the `mingluo.me` DNS provider point apex to GitHub Pages — A records `185.199.108.153`, `185.199.109.153`, `185.199.110.153`, `185.199.111.153` (+ AAAA `2606:50c0:8000::153` … `8003::153`); re-enter the custom domain in the `Mingling94.github.io` repo's Pages settings; wait for cert provisioning; then enable **Enforce HTTPS**.

## Permission justifications

| Permission | Justification |
|-----------|---------------|
| activeTab | Read the current page's text and images to check for AI-generated content |
| storage | Store user preferences, cached scores, and local AI exposure history |
| host_permissions (API server) | Look up AI detection scores and community votes |
| optional: tabs | Update the toolbar badge when switching between tabs |
| optional: host_permissions (all URLs) | Allow configuring a custom API server |
