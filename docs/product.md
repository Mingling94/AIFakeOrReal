# Product Overview

## The Problem

Your social media feeds are filling up with AI-generated content — fake images, bot-written posts, AI art passed off as real, ChatGPT-generated comments. It's getting harder to tell what's real and what's not. Most people don't have the time or expertise to spot the difference.

## What AI Fake Or Real Does

**AI Fake Or Real is a browser extension that tells you if what you're looking at on social media was made by AI.** It works in the background while you scroll — no clicking, no copying text into a website, no interruption to your browsing.

Think of it like an ad blocker, but for AI-generated content.

## Who It's For

- **Social media users** who want to know what's real in their feed
- **News readers** who want to verify articles aren't AI-generated clickbait
- **Parents** concerned about their kids consuming AI-fabricated content
- **Anyone** tired of AI slop flooding Reddit, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, X, and YouTube

## Who It's NOT For

This is not a plagiarism checker. It's not for teachers grading essays or editors reviewing manuscripts. Those are different tools (GPTZero, Originality.ai, Turnitin). AI Fake Or Real is for **everyday browsing** — scrolling your feed and knowing what's real.

## How It Feels

### You see a badge. That's it.
When you browse, a small badge appears on the extension icon:
- 🟢 **✓** — Real. This content looks human-made.
- 🟡 **?** — Unclear. Could go either way.
- 🔴 **AI** — AI Fake. This was probably generated by AI.

You don't have to do anything. It just works.

### Want more detail? Click the icon.
The popup shows:
- **"REAL"** or **"AI FAKE"** verdict with confidence %
- **Why** — "Natural vocabulary", "AI vocabulary detected", etc.
- **What the community thinks** — other users' votes on this content
- **Your AI exposure** — what % of your recent browsing has been AI content

### Scrolling social media? Badges appear on posts.
On Reddit, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok, and X, small inline badges appear directly on posts that look AI-generated. No need to click — the suspect posts are marked as you scroll.

## Key Features

### 🔍 Passive Detection
Works automatically on every page. No clicking "scan". The badge updates as you browse.

### 👥 Community Votes
Not sure if something is AI? See what other users think. Vote 👍 Real or 👎 AI Fake. When the scanner and community disagree, you'll see an ⚡ alert — your vote helps settle it.

### 📊 AI Exposure Stats
See how much of your browsing is AI-generated content — over the last day, week, month, or year. It's like screen time, but for AI content. All data stays on your device.

### 🌐 Works Everywhere
Reddit, Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, TikTok, X (Twitter), news sites, blogs — any website.

### 🔒 Private
No browsing history collected. The local scanner runs entirely in your browser. No cookies, no tracking, no ads. Open source so you can verify.

### 💰 Free Forever
No subscription, no credit card, no word limits. Free and open source.

## Detection Pipeline

The extension uses three layers, from fastest to most accurate:

1. **Local scanner** (instant, private) — catches obvious AI patterns in vocabulary and sentence structure. Runs entirely in your browser.
2. **Community signal** — aggregates votes from other users who've seen the same content. More votes = higher confidence.
3. **LLM analysis** (when needed) — sends text/images to AI models for deep analysis. Results are cached for 1 week so the same content is only analyzed once across all users.
